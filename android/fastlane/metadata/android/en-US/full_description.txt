TrackMauzo

TrackMauzo is an offline point-of-sale and business management app for small shops and growing stores.
It helps you manage sales, inventory, customers, receipts, reports, and store operations in one place.

Key features:

* Fast checkout and sales tracking
* Inventory monitoring and product management
* Customer records and staff tools
* Receipt printing and sharing
* Reports, insights, and business summaries

TrackMauzo is designed to keep your day-to-day shop operations simple, organized, and easy to review.
